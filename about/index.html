Community Networking Togetherness

Meet the TunedIn crew: Ben Whitmore, Paul Winstanley, and David Brook. These three tech buddies and seasoned speakers noticed something missing in the UK tech scene over the past couple of years; no all-day events for folks into Endpoint Management or Microsoft 365 (including Azure). So, they thought, ‘Why not start our own?’ And that’s exactly what they did. They kicked things off with a bang in London in December 2023, and now they’re all about taking this show on the road, planning to bring more cool events to tech heads across the UK. It’s all about sharing knowledge, having fun, and connecting people who love tech as much as they do.
